Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts aiming to foster innovation and collaboration. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global tech conferences, attracting thousands of attendees and featuring pioneering advancements in AI, robotics, and sustainable tech.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we aim to inspire innovation, connect minds, and shape the future of technology through inclusive dialogue, visionary thinking, and practical application. We are committed to diversity, sustainability, and creating impactful technological solutions.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lin

AI pioneer and advocate for ethical machine learning. Dr. Lin shared groundbreaking work on bias mitigation in algorithms during her 2021 keynote.

Raj Patel

Tech entrepreneur and founder of GreenCircuits, Raj Patel explored the intersection of technology and environmental sustainability in 2022.

Sophia Clarke

Cybersecurity strategist known for her innovative approaches to digital privacy. Clarke led a panel on zero-trust frameworks in 2023.
